Spiritual Importance

The Kashi Vishwanath Mandir is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and holds a special place in the hearts of devotees. Rebuilt several times, it is a symbol of resilience and devotion to Lord Shiva.

  • Dedicated to Lord Shiva
  • Ancient structure with rich history
  • One of the holiest places in India

Temple Features & Surroundings

  • Gold-plated Shikhara and domes
  • Near Manikarnika Ghat
  • New Kashi Corridor and Ganga view
  • Spiritual and cultural vibrancy

Festivals & Celebrations

  • Mahashivratri with grand ceremonies
  • Daily aartis on the Ghat and temple
  • Spiritual discourses and kirtans
